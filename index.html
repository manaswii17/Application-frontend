Common Questions

How long does the battery last?
The battery typically lasts 4-6 hours depending on field conditions and usage intensity.
What to do if machine gets stuck?
Use the emergency stop button, clear the obstruction, and restart. Contact support if problem persists.
How often should I clean the machine?
Clean after each use, especially the cutting blades and sensors. Deep cleaning weekly.
